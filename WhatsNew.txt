GreenCubic 1.5.5.4.2

############ENGLISH######################################

Control:
Arrows.
LEFT, RIGHT - movement left, right.
UP - jump or move up on ladder.
DOWN - go down from the object or the ladder (to enter the basement, you must stay on trapdoor and press DOWN)
LSHIFT - running.
D - drink
E - eat
ESC - pause
